Game Rules:

  1. Each player can move 3 spaces horizontally or vertically.
  2. If a player passes over a box containing a weapon, they drop their current weapon and replace it with the new one.
  3. If players cross over adjacent squares (horizontally or vertically), a battle begins.
  4. Each player plays takes a turn. The player can choose to attack or defend against the next shot.
  5. When one of the players loses all lifePoints, the game is over.
